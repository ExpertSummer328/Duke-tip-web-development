About Us

Hi! Our names are Aidan Benjamin, Jackson Cline, Scott Davis, and Arman Tavallaei, and we are the creators of Jabr. We all met in San Antonio, Texas, when we were going into 8th grade. We all met in a Web Applications Develpoment class that we all signed up for through Duke TIP. We are all from 4 different states, and we all came together to make this amazing website. Here is a little bit about each of us:

Jabr is a page that we made for our Web Applications Development class at Trinity University for Duke TIP. Our purpose for this is to create a website to allow people to talk with people all over the world in an environment where everybody can have fun and enjoy others. We want people to talk to whoever they want whenever they want without having to go and find a phone or pay for expensive international minutes. We want everybody to have good, clean fun on this website by talking to anybody they want. Have fun on Jabr!
